Privacy Policy

Wordly

Last updated: 05/05/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Slonapps (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use the mobile application Wordly (the “App”).

Summary

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • The App helps users learn English vocabulary through lessons, word practice, and progress tracking.
  • We may use standard analytics and crash reports to improve stability, performance, and learning experience.
  • If subscriptions or in-app purchases are added in the future, payments will be processed by Apple.

Information We Process

Learning Progress

The App may store information about your learning activity, such as completed lessons, studied words, selected level, answers, streaks, and progress in order to provide the App’s features.

App Preferences

The App may store your selected language, learning level, daily goal, notification preferences, and other settings needed to personalize your learning experience.

Device and App Data

We may process basic technical information such as device type, OS version, app version, language, and crash logs to diagnose issues and improve the App.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App may send reminders related to learning, lessons, daily practice, or progress. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

Purchases and Subscriptions

The App currently may be available without subscriptions or in-app purchases. If paid features, subscriptions, or in-app purchases are added in the future, transactions will be processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment details, such as credit card numbers.

How We Use Information

  • To provide vocabulary lessons and practice features.
  • To save your learning progress and app preferences.
  • To personalize the learning experience.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To respond to support requests.

Data Sharing

We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the App, such as analytics or crash reporting providers. These providers are permitted to process data only for providing services to us.

We do not sell your personal data.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy. Learning progress and app preferences may remain stored on your device or in the App until you delete the App or reset your progress, where available.

Your Choices

  • Notifications: You can enable or disable notifications in iOS Settings.
  • Analytics: If your device supports it, you can limit ad or analytics tracking in iOS privacy settings.
  • App data: You can delete the App to remove locally stored app data from your device.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, or the minimum age required in your country. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

If you use the App from outside your country, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above.

Privacy Policy

Wordly

Last updated: 11/05/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Slonapps (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use the mobile application Wordly (the “App”).

Summary

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • The App helps users learn English vocabulary through lessons, word practice, and progress tracking.
  • We may use standard analytics and crash reports to improve stability, performance, and learning experience.
  • The App offers optional subscriptions and in-app purchases processed securely by Apple.

Information We Process

Learning Progress

The App may store information about your learning activity, such as completed lessons, studied words, selected level, answers, streaks, and progress in order to provide the App’s features.

App Preferences

The App may store your selected language, learning level, daily goal, notification preferences, and other settings needed to personalize your learning experience.

Device and App Data

We may process basic technical information such as device type, OS version, app version, language, and crash logs to diagnose issues and improve the App.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App may send reminders related to learning, lessons, daily practice, or progress. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

Purchases and Subscriptions

The App offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions and/or in-app purchases that unlock premium features. All payments and subscription management are handled securely by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive or store your full payment information, such as credit card numbers. We may receive limited information related to purchases and subscription status in order to provide premium access and restore purchases.

How We Use Information

  • To provide vocabulary lessons and practice features.
  • To save your learning progress and app preferences.
  • To personalize the learning experience.
  • To provide premium features and restore purchases.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To respond to support requests.
